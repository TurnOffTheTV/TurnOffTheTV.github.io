What is Extra Frog?

Extra Frog is a Discord bot where you can fight other people with text.
The outcome is weighted random by applied armor, weapons, level, etc. Get Frog Credits through various means and upgrade and buy new stuff. Get XP from fighting to level up; each level up gives you Frog Credits.

How does Extra Frog work?

Extra Frog is luck-based at the beginning, but as you get better it is more about the items you collect.
Fighting someone at a higher level is risky as they may have better armor or weapons, but you will get better rewards.
You should try not to fight someone of a lower level, they will yield less profit.
At the beginning, you have to get 5 XP to level up, then 10, then 20, then 40, etc.
There is always lots of new armor and weapons, and there is nowhere (officially) you can find a full list.

Server control people info

If you add Extra Frog, setting its read messages access to a channel stops it from responding there.
Even though it wants administrator privellages, it doesn't do anything with it.
