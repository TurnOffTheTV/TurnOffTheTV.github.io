Wirebox

A simple physics-based game that I made to be played offline.

Controls

'a' and 'd' to move

'space' to jump.



FAQ

Q: Why can't I move when I jump?

A: You need momentum to move. You have to get a running start.


Q: I'm stuck in an endless falling loop! Help!

A: That's not really a question, but anyway... Press 'r' to reset to the start of the level.
